Prefix & Suffix Lesson

🔧 Breaking Down "UNCOMFORTABLE"

UN-
Prefix meaning "NOT"
unhappy, unfair
COMFORT
Root word
ease, relief
-ABLE
Suffix meaning "can be"
readable, doable

UN + COMFORT + ABLE = "not able to be comfortable" ✅

Parts of Speech Review

NOUN

ADVERB

Advanced Sequence Concepts

🔄 Beyond Simple Time Order:

Cause & Effect: One event CAUSES another
Problem → Solution: Issues get solved step-by-step
Emotional Journey: Feelings change over time
Internal vs External: Thoughts AND actions in sequence

Advanced Signal Words: Meanwhile, Simultaneously, Previously, Afterwards

"Letting Go" - Title Analysis

🎯 Why This Title Matters:

  • 📖 Literal meaning: Alex must physically release something
  • 💭 Symbolic meaning: He must give up what he wants
  • 🔄 Process meaning: Letting go happens in stages
  • ⚖️ Choice meaning: Some decisions require sacrifice

Notice how the SEQUENCE shows us Alex's internal struggle! 🧠

Sequence Creates Suspense

📈 How Authors Build Tension Through Time:

Setup: Alex faces the challenge
Complication: The choice becomes harder
Crisis: Alex must decide NOW
Resolution: He makes his choice

Each step makes us more worried about Alex! 😰
