Point of View Quick Guide

First Person

I, me, my

Character tells their own story

"I looked at myself and was shocked"

Third Person Limited

He, she, they

Narrator shows ONE character's thoughts

"Alex found himself emaciated"

Third Person Omniscient

He, she, they

Narrator knows ALL characters' thoughts

"Alex worried while Dan secretly planned"

Objective/Dramatic

He, she, they

Only actions and dialogue, no thoughts

"Alex sat down. Dan walked away."
